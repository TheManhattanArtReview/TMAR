The Manhattan Art Job Opening

During the month of September The Manhattan Art Review will be accepting applications from writers interested in contributing to The Manhattan Art Review.
- Applicants may send three writing samples to hankjwimbleton@gmail.com.
- Selected applicants will be contacted and given the month of October to write five reviews of art exhibitions currently on display, as well as a small stipend.
- Adequate review submissions may lead to opportunities for further commissions.

Applicants need not be located in New York City, but access to a large selection of galleries is a practical necessity.

This position does not need to be filled.
